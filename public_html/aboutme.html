Zachary F. Wyman

    Currently a sophomore at Clemson University, Zach Wyman is studying Computer Science with a minor in Finance. Outside of the classroom, he is involved in numerous organizations throughout campus. His Leadership positions include being a member of the cabinet of three organizations at Clemson University. He is the treasurer for the CU CyberSecurity Club, the corresponding secretary for the fraternity of Phi Gamma Delta, and the VP of Programming for Order of Omega. In addition, he is a member of the Spiro Center for Entreprenuerial Leadership Advisory Board and the PR committee for Student Government.
    Having grown up in the city of Clemson, Zach also works with the community. His research involves the use of Catapult monitors with the Clemson Football team, which he has been involved in since the beginning of his time at Clemson. In addition, he works closely with Safe Harbor SC, an organization dedicated to preventing domestic violence and sheltering battered women in the upstate. Lastly, he has had multiple internships throughout the world and at home, ranging from Kaiserslautern, Germany to Charleston, SC, back home to Clemson, SC.
